# Cumulant Generating Function

$$
\begin{align}
K(t) &= \ln E[e^{tX}] \\
&= \sum_{n=1}^\infty \kappa_n \frac{t^n}{n!}
\end{align}
$$
is the log of moment generating function. 

$\kappa_n$ is the $n$th **cumulant**.

- $\kappa_1 = E[X]$
- $\kappa_2 = var[X]$
- $\kappa_3 = E[(X - E[X])^3]$
